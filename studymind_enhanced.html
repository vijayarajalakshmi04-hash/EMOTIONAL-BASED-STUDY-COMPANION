Features

Everything You Need to Study with Emotional Intelligence

From face detection to AI chatbot and voice input — StudyMind is your all-in-one emotion-aware study platform.

🎭
Real-Time Emotion Detection
Using your webcam and face-api.js, StudyMind detects your facial expression and maps it to one of 5 emotional states every few seconds.
😤 Stressed 😄 Happy 😴 Tired 😢 Sad 😐 Neutral
🤖
AI Study Companion Chatbot
Powered by Claude AI, your companion understands your mood, answers study questions, and gives motivational support — all in under 3 sentences.
🎙️
Voice Input & Output
Speak to your study companion using the Web Speech API. Your voice is transcribed, sent to the AI, and the reply is spoken back using text-to-speech.
📊
Mood & Productivity Dashboard
Track your emotional state over time with beautiful charts. See how your moods correlate with study hours and performance improvements.
📋
Smart Study Suggestions
Based on your detected emotion, StudyMind suggests the right kind of study: deep focus, light review, creative work, or a short break.
🔔
Study Reminders & Streaks
Set notification reminders and maintain study streaks. Your companion celebrates your consistency and nudges you when you fall behind.
